E85 Flex-Fuel

  • ✅ Lowest CO₂/km (0.070 kg)

  • ❌ Highest cost/km (₹6.37)
  • ❌ Lower mileage (12.9 km/l)

Diesel

  • ✅ Strong mileage (19.6 km/l)
  • ✅ Fast refuel time
  • ❌ Highest maintenance/km
  • ❌ Costs climb steeply with age
🚗 Best for: highway/fleet high-mileage use

CNG

  • ✅ Low cost/km (₹3.32)
  • ✅ Low CO₂ output
  • ❌ 8-min refuel, fewer stations
  • ❌ Moderate maintenance
🚗 Best for: budget city commuters

Electric (EV)

  • ✅ Lowest cost/km (₹1.75)
  • ✅ Lowest maintenance/km
  • ❌ Long 45-min recharge
  • ❌ Needs charging infrastructure
🚗 Best for: home-charging city dwellers
