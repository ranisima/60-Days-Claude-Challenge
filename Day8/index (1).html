        Air quality sources: aqi.in Real-Time Most Polluted City Ranking (12 Jul 2026); aqi.in city dashboards for Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru (Jul 2026); IQAir India country page; CPCB National AQI Bulletin.

        Water quality sources: BIS IS 10500:2012 11-city compliance study (via Nexus Water); TapWaterScan India Report; Care Dale India Water Hardness Map; regional TDS/hardness surveys (2025–26).

        Methodology & data quality note: AQI values reflect the most recent stable real-time readings available at time of analysis (US AQI scale, 0–500) and were validated for range (0–500) and completeness — all 15 cities have complete records, 0 missing values. Indian NAQI category labels (Good→Severe) are applied to these AQI figures for consistency with national reporting conventions. PM2.5 is derived mathematically from AQI using standard EPA breakpoint interpolation; PM10 is estimated as PM2.5 × 1.75, a typical ratio observed in dust-influenced Indian urban air — both are modeled estimates, not direct sensor readings. Water Quality Scores are composite estimates (0–100) synthesized from published TDS/hardness data, groundwater assessments, and drinking-water compliance findings; they are not live sensor feeds. This tool is for educational awareness, not a medical or regulatory diagnostic — consult local authorities/CPCB for official readings.
